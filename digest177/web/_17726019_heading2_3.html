AC Soft Starter Products

Enclosed Altistart™ 22 Motor Controllers

Enclosed Altistart 22 (ATS22) solid-state combination motor controllers are a pre-engineered, integrated solution for reduced voltage starting and soft stopping of standard three-phase asynchronous induction (squirrel cage) motors. The Enclosed 22 controllers consist of a disconnect means and an ATS22 softstarter in a stand-alone enclosure. Enclosed 22 controllers integrate the ATS22 softstart technology into a combination package for application requirements up to 400 hp at 460 V.

  • 3–150 hp, 208 V

  • 5–200 hp, 230 V

  • 10–400 hp, 460 V

  • 15–500 hp, 575V
