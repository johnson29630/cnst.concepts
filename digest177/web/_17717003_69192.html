Combination Starters Units with Motor Circuit Protector Disconnects

Model 6 NEMA-rated FVNR combination starter units use PowerPact™ Motor Circuit Protectors.

Ratings: 480 V, NEMA 12, Type 1B-D wiring, 100,000 AIR. Units include 1 N.O./1 N.C. auxiliary contacts. Units with pilot devices use 22 mm type. Units without pilot devices include a station plate with knockouts for five 22 mm devices.

Thermal units are not included with melting alloy overloads.
