Footnotes

  1. Buoyancy data is calculated for use in water. Consult factory for buoyancy data in media with a different specific gravity than water.
    When ordering float accessories, first specify the desired float accessory package, such as 9049A6 or 9049A6CS, then as a second item give the number of additional rod kits required. For example, for a 9049A6 with 15 ft of rod, order as follows:
    Item A = 9049A6, quantity = 1; Item B = 9049T1, quantity = 4.
  2. Net buoyancy of float has been calculated with float 80% submerged, thus allowing 20% factor of safety.
  3. Compensating spring not effective in combination with Short lever length position.
