Current Transformers

Class 5820

Round Split-Core Current Transformer (CT) Selection:

Two remote current transformers (CT) are required for three phase loads. Three CT’s are required for networks with line to neutral loads. Depending on installation, additional CTs may be required. Additional sizes are available.
