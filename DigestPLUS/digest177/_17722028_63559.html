Renewal Parts for Class 9012–9038 Devices

Renewal parts are generally available for Pump Control Products with a numerical date code—for example, 172 (first quarter, 1972)—or a current date code. Parts are no longer available for devices manufactured before 1965.

To order, specify the Class and Type number of the kit.
