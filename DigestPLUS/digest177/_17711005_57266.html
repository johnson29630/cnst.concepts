Ordering Information

  1. Service section: Order service section from Single Main Circuit Breaker with Distribution (Series E4) , Single Main Fusible Disconnect with Distribution (Series E4) , or Multiple Mains—Fusible (Series E4) , as determined by mains rating, voltage, and system.

  2. Meter doors: Order meter door from Meter Door Selection as determined by the height and utility metering requirements.

  3. Accessories and subfeeds: Order as required from Accessories and/or Subfeed Circuit Breakers (Series E4) .

  4. Circuit breakers and switches: Order devices from pages listed below as determined by voltage, trip rating, AIR, and mounting space.

Multiple Mains and Branch Devices

  • QO, QOB, QO-VH, QOB-VH: Plug-On Circuit Breakers and QOB Bolt-On Circuit Breakers

  • I-Line: F-frame, PowerPact Q-frame for I-Line™ Panelboards and Switchboards to PowerPact P- and R-frame for I-Line™ Panelboards and Switchboards

  • QMB Switches: QMB Fusible Branch Switches

Footnotes

  1. 6-jaw meter socket can also be used on 4- and 5-jaw applications.
  2. Door with provisions for mounting meter socket.
  3. All EUSERC Utilities (except Arizona Public Service and Salt River Project) require a lug landing kit SA8LL.
  4. To be used on 120/240 V, 3Ø4W delta applications.
  5. Two pole circuit breaker catalog numbers are completed by adding required phase connection letters as suffix (for example, SASFBH100LAC).
  6. Cannot use subfeed circuit breaker kit with multiple mains service section switchboards.
