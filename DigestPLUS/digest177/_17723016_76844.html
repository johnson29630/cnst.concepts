TeSys™ D IEC Style Instantaneous Control Relays

CAD32

These 600 V relays are approved for use around the world. TeSys D relays are usually mounted on 35 mm DIN track, but can also be mounted directly to a panel. The contacts have NEMA A600 and Q600 ratings, in addition to the standard IEC ratings, making them suitable for use in most any control circuit. Low consumption versions are available for use with low level DC control signals from a computer or a PLC. Adder decks can be added to a basic five pole relay to make it up to an 11 pole relay. The serrated silver-nickel contacts with wiping action provide excellent reliability in 12 or 24 V control circuits. Special auxiliary contacts are available for switching low power down to 5 V at 10 mA. Timer and mechanical latch attachments are available.
