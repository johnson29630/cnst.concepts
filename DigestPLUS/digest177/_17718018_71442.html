TeSys™ F AC Coils (For LC1F115, F150, F185, F225, F265, F330, F400, F500, F630, F780, and F800)

LX1 coils are the standard coils that are included when a voltage code is added to the contactor part number. The LX9 coils may be ordered separately for special applications. LX9 coils do not include a built-in normally open holding circuit contact; a separate auxiliary contact block with a N.O. contact should be added to the contactor. Both the LX1 and LX9 coils can be used on the previous F-line contactors.
