Refer to Catalog 9014CT0201

XMLK Pressure Transmitters

XMLK•••••D

M12 Connector

XMLK•••••P

Metri-Pack Connector

Type XMLK pressure transmitters are characterized by their ceramic pressure-measuring cell. The deformation caused by the pressure is transmitted to the resistors of a Wheatstone bridge silk-screened on the ceramic. The change in resistance is then processed by the integrated electronics to provide an analog output signal.
