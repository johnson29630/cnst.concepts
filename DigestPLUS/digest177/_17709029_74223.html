  3. 3P circuit breakers with this trip unit can be used for 2P applications.
  4. AL400L61K3 terminal wire ranges are (1) 2 AWG–600 kcmil Cu or (1) 2 AWG–500 kcmil Al.

  7. 600 V interrupt ratings not available for D-frame.
