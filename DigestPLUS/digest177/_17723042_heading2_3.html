ABR

Refer to Catalog DIA3ED2090304EN-US

Electromechanical Interface Modules

ABR1E411F

ABR electromechanical relay modules are for discrete digital input or output control signals exchanged in automated equipment. Features include:

  • High contact reliability

  • 5 separate character places for marking

  • LED indication of the control signal state

  • 35 mm DIN 3 or 32 mm DIN 1 track mountable
