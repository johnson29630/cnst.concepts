  1. Can be fitted with additional contact blocks, see Add-On Contact Block (with screw clamp terminal connections) .

  3. Order bulb separately, see BA9s Bulbs and Associated Accessories.

  8. Cannot stack additional contact blocks onto these blocks.
  9. Additional blocks cannot be attached to the back of these contact blocks or light modules. However, spring terminal contact blocks can be mounted behind screw terminal contact blocks.
