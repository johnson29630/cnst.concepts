Class 5600 / Refer to Catalog 5600CT9101

Powerbus Plug-In Units

Powerbus plug-in units are rated maximum 100 A and may be offered as field installable or factory assembled units. All units conform to NEMA type 1. An optional kit is available for FA and QO units to raise the protection to IP54. This kit raises the QOR unit to moisture protection of IPX3.
