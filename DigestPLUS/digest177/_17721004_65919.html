Make-before-break (overlapping) SPDT
The normally open contact closes before the normally closed contact opens.

Break-before-make (offset) SPDT
The normally closed contact opens before the normally open contact closes.

Simultaneous make and break—SPDT
The normally closed contact opens at the same time as the normally open contact closes.
