Miniature MS Limit Switch

9007MS

The heavy-duty, miniature MS limit switch is completely encapsulated and intended for difficult applications such as machine tools, earth moving equipment, and general transportation. 9007MS04S0084


The switch has 40 mm mtg hole centers.
