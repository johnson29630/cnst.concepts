Ventilation Systems with Filters

Specially recommended for installations in which the ambient temperature is lower than the desired temperature inside the enclosure, a high protection rating is required: IP54 or IP55, and the surrounding environment is relatively clean, allowing air to enter the enclosure.

  • 38 to 850 m3/h.

  • Five input voltages: AC: 400/440 V, 230 V, 115 V (50/60 Hz), DC: 48 V and 24 V.

  • Broad range of accessories (filters, IP55 and EMC covers, anti-vandalism kit).

  • UL Listing: E80264

Thermal Control

Thermostats control the temperature inside the enclosure and send a signal when maximum or minimum temperature values have been reached.

  • Temperature control: adjustable thermostats; single or double.

  • Relative humidity control: adjustable humidistat.

  • Temperature and relative humidity control: adjustable hygrotherm.
